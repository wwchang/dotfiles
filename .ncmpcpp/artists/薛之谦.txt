Jacky Xue Zhiqian is a mandopop singer/songwriter and MY SHOW 2005 talent show semifinalist. He composes many songs in his albums. Won the best stage performer in mainland at Music Radio Awards 2009. 

Song: Chai Tou Feng 钗头凤(Phoenix head hairpin-literal meaning). The title and parts of the lyrics are based on the ancient Chinese poem from the Song Dynasty Songwriter: Jacky Xue Debuted with his first album in 2006
