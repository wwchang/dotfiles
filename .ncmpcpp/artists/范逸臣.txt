Van Fan/范逸臣 is an Amis singer and actor best known for his role in  Cape No. 7, the second top-selling film in Taiwanese cinematic history. Since his debut in 2002, Van has risen to high fame within the Greater China Area.

To date, Van has released 6 albums, contributed towards 15 movie/drama soundtracks, and starred in 7 acting roles. He is also the lead singer for two bands.
