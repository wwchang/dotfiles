Hu Ge is an Chinese actor, born in Shanghai, who also has sung for shows he was in. He debuted in the TV series "Dandelion (Pu Gong Ying)" in 2003, and was propelled to fame by his lead role as Li Xiao Yao in the game-to-TV-series "Chinese Paladin (Xian Jian Qi Xia Zhuan)", where he sang two songs on the soundtrack. In the 2005 TV series "Fairy from Wonderland (Tian Wai Fei Xian)", he was the main male lead, Dong Yong, and again sang two songs for the show. In 2008, he also sang for the ending theme of The Eagle Shooting Heroes, where he acted as Guo Jing.
